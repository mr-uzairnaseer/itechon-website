    Define Requirements

    2

    Sourcing Talent

    Leverage job portals, AI communities, and professional networks to find qualified candidates.

    3

    Screening Candidates

    Conduct an initial vetting process to assess resumes and portfolios for relevant AI project experience and knowledge of AI tools.

    4

    Technical Assessments

    Use coding challenges and data engineering tasks to evaluate problem-solving skills and technical proficiency in AI development.

    5

    Behavioral Interviews

    Conduct behavioral interviews to understand the candidate’s teamwork, adaptability, and approach to problem-solving.

    7

    Onboarding Process

    Provide a detailed onboarding plan to introduce the developer to your workflows, tools, and project goals, ensuring a smooth integration.

    By following these steps, you can effectively hire skilled AI developers who align with your technical and organizational needs.
