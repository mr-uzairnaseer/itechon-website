    What is the difference between eCommerce and mCommerce?

    eCommerce refers to electronic commerce conducted primarily on desktops or laptops. In contrast, mCommerce, or mobile commerce, enables buying and selling via mobile devices. With the rise of mobile-optimized websites and PWAs, the line between them is now very thin, and they are often used interchangeably in practice.

    What type of eCommerce development do you do?

    An on-demand delivery app allows fast, real-time delivery of goods directly to customers' doorsteps. It supports modern business models that focus on immediate customer needs. We develop offshore on-demand delivery solutions with built-in analytics.
